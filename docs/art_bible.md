# The Dream of the Two Princesses Art Bible

This file is the consistency target for all future generated art. Scene art should be rejected or regenerated when it drifts from these rules.

## Current Reference Assets

- Princess reference: `res://assets/reference/princesses_reference.png`
- Dragon reference: `res://assets/reference/dragons_reference.png`
- Animal reference: pending generation.
- Scene replacements should be prompted against this document and visually checked against these reference sheets before replacing shipped backgrounds.

## Global Style

- Medium: polished 3D CGI children's storybook illustration.
- Mood: magical, warm, brave, whimsical, never frightening.
- Lighting: cinematic but soft, with readable faces and strong silhouettes.
- Format: landscape 16:9 game backgrounds.
- UI space: preserve open areas near the bottom or edges for story text, buttons, and click targets.
- Avoid: visible text, watermarks, modern objects, horror styling, distorted hands, inconsistent ages, inconsistent costumes.

## Emily

- Role: playable protagonist.
- Apparent age: young child/princess storybook heroine.
- Hair: long, wavy light brown hair, warm chestnut highlights, usually loose.
- Outfit: vibrant pink princess dress with gentle gold embroidery and sparkly detail.
- Accessories: small gold tiara; carries the ornate golden magic mirror after the bedroom scene.
- Personality on screen: brave, curious, kind, resourceful.
- Consistency checks: pink dress remains pink, hair remains light brown and long, face remains round and childlike, never becomes a teenager or adult.

## Audrey

- Role: Emily's sister, rescued princess.
- Hair: dark brown wavy shoulder-length hair.
- Eyes: dark brown.
- Outfit: blue princess dress with elegant details.
- Accessories: small gold necklace.
- Personality on screen: cautious, gentle, brave.
- Consistency checks: blue dress remains blue, hair stays darker and shorter than Emily's, necklace is visible when close enough.

## Jax

- Role: Emily's dragon companion.
- Body: friendly dragon with shimmering pink and light blue scales.
- Wings: butterfly-like wings with pink and blue patterns.
- Face: large expressive eyes, kind and nurturing.
- Glow: gentle magical glow, not fiery or aggressive.
- Consistency checks: reads as pink-first, with blue accents; never scary, spiky, or reptilian-realistic.

## Lily

- Role: Audrey's dragon companion.
- Body: friendly dragon in various shades of blue.
- Wings: butterfly-like wings with blue patterns and soft glow.
- Face: calm, protective, kind.
- Consistency checks: reads as blue-first; similar design language to Jax but clearly distinct.

## Animal Friends

- Bear: small warm yellow bear, round soft features, carries or loves honey, cheerful and gentle.
- Donkey: gray donkey with droopy ears and a lovable gloomy expression.
- Rabbit: white rabbit with tall uneven ears, slightly anxious but friendly.
- Consistency checks: animals should stay storybook-classic and companion-sized; avoid realistic wildlife proportions.

## Locations

- Bedroom: elegant pink room, plush canopy bed, moon-and-stars blanket, white window frames, desk with quill, fashion mannequin, chandelier, golden magic mirror.
- Meadow and Castle: rolling wildflower hills, silver stream, white stone castle inspired by Neuschwanstein, blue/purple spires, mountains, magical sparkles.
- Great Forest: dense trees, glowing blue mushrooms, lush plants, child-safe mystery, crooked witch house with warm orange window glow.
- Dragon Cave: crystal cave behind ivy and stone, glowing pink/blue crystals, round gate, Jax and Lily on crystal beds.
- Tower/Castle Rescue: white stone tower balcony, blue roof/spires, night sky, Emily and Audrey reunited.

## Scene Acceptance Checklist

- Emily matches the reference in hair, dress, age, and accessories.
- Audrey matches the reference whenever present.
- Jax and Lily keep the same wing language and color identity.
- The bear, donkey, and rabbit keep the same proportions and expressions.
- Scene composition leaves room for Godot UI and click/tap targets.
- No unwanted text appears in the image.
- The image works at 1280x720 after cropping or scaling.
